## What is SHAMA?

  - Token Name : **Shawarma** 
  - Token Symbol: **SHAMA**
  - Max Token Supply: **210,000 SHAMA**

SHAMA is a **Real World Business (RWB)**. Instead of unknown hidden assets or debt, SHAMA is backed by physical fast food shops selling finger-licking shawarma and other delicious items. Every SHAMA token equals 1 mouth-watering Shawarma and can be redeemed for a meal at any of the shops.

The SHAMA ecosystem phase 1 consists of 55 shops and will be built over **36 months**, pushing the token price from **5\$** per SHAMA in the Genesys Pool (Pool One) to over **\$300** per SHAMA in the Degen Pool (Pool  10).
SHAMA is a low supply (210K Max Supply), High Yield (550K USDT) system. With 70K tokens locked for CEX listing SHAMA has just 140K token in circulation.

## The 10-Pool Pricing Model

SHAMA uses a **10-pool model** to emit SHAMA tokens to the public. The 10 pools strategy ensures a near three year dedicated marketing campaign to onboard new users to the ecosystem while pushing growth forward. Each pool is also a milestone to earmark the establishment of a corresponding number of the 55 physical fast food shops. So in pool 1, 1 shop is created, in pool 2, 2 shops are created, and so on. The pool token pricing follows an exponential curve — rewarding early participants and reflecting the reduced risk as more shops become operational.

## Strategy
### Uniswap Price Rebalancing

When each pool stage opens, the SHAMA team will buy SHAMA to rebalance the uniswap pair to match the planned pool prices. This depends on if the uniswap price is above / below the sale price. If the price is above the sell price (as planned), No rebalancing, if price is below, the pair will be rebalanced to match the pool sale price.


### High-Incentive Staking

By matching the sale amount with staking rewards, each pool offers strong initial APR. This encourages holders to stake rather than sell while the price climbs toward $300.

### Phased Exchange Listing

SHAMA is planned for listing on **Binance, Bybit, Bitget, Coinbase, Kraken, KuCoin, and OKX**. The strategy is to build volume on Tier-2 exchanges, then move to Tier-1 listings, - using the 70,000 token CEX reserve.
