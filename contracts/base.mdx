### Pool Configuration

All pools share the following staking parameters:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Minimum Stake | 5 SHAMA |
| Minimum Staking Period | 180 days |

Genesys (Pool 1) has a sale cap of **1,500 SHAMA**. Exodyne–Degen (Pools 2–10) each have a sale cap of **3,000 SHAMA**.
